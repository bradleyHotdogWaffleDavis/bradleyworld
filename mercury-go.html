Mercury Go

CLIENTMercury

PLATFORMNative iOS & Android

MY ROLELead UX/UI Designer

MVP DEVLIVERYMay 2017 - Jun 2018

Pushing for positivity, we delivered Mercury an app that stands out. Our goal was to inspire customers about energy by offering something meaningful & beneficial that they can proudly get behind, instead of the standard utility-focused app.

EARNCustomers can earn Mercury Dollars & win prizes by completing a range of different challenges.

STEP CHALLENGESEnter & complete step challenges by using your human energy.

STEP COUNTERWatch your steps count up in real-time as you take on step challenges.

FRIEND GET FRIENDGet your friends involved using Mercury Go's simple refer-a-friend function that uses the device's native share function.

RISE & SHAKE YOUR LAKEInspired by hydro, Mercury's main source of energy generation. Any Mercury Dollars you earn is represented by the rise of your “real physics” digital lake level, controlled by your phone's gyroscope.

SPENDSpend Mercury Dollars to reduce your power bill or fly faster by converting them into Airpoints.

MERCURY DOLLARS1 Mercury Dollar is equivalent to 1 NZD, or 1 Airpoints.

YOUR DIGITAL LAKEStart spending by dragging the scrubber to watch your lake level drop.

MY ACCOUNTWe worked closely with Mercury’s API team to improve & implement new 'My Account' functionality, allowing us to empower customers in different ways about their usage.

YOUR BILLView & pay your bill balance faster.

MULTIPLE ACCOUNTSEasily swap between your Mercury accounts.

USAGEMake smarter decisions about your power consumption by swipe & tapping the graphs to see your bill & daily usage breakdowns.

ONBOARDINGEmpowering customers to explore & discover, so they can do all the important things faster.

LOGIN SCREENSCustomer or not, get the bigger picture & see what Mercury Go has to offer.

DISCOVERY HELPERSHelping customer's discovery through Mercury Go & it's key functionality, "Discovery Helpers" appear throughout.

We continue to plan, prioritise & deliver an outcome focused roadmap improvements, within 2 week sprints.
