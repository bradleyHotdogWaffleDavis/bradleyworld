Mercury Go

CLIENTMercury

PLATFORMNative iOS & Android app

MY ROLELead UX/UI Designer

MVP DEVLIVERYMay 2017 - Jun 2018

Creating a positive brand worth talking about. We delivered Mercury an app that stands out. We focused on inspiring customers about energy & offering them something purposeful & beneficial, that they can proudly get behind rather than a typical utility focused app like all the other power companies.

EARNCustomers can earn Mercury Dollars & win prizes by using their own human energy to complete a range of different challenges.

STEP CHALLENGESEnter & complete step challenges by using your own human energy.

STEP COUNTERWatch your steps being counted in real-time as you take on step challenges.

FRIEND GET FRIENDGet your friends in on the fun by joining Mercury through Mercury Go's simple referral mechanic using the devices native share function.

RAISE & SHAKE YOUR LAKEInspired by Mercury’s close relationship to the Waikato River, any Mercury Dollars you earn is represented by raising your “real physics” digital water level, that’s controlled by your phones gyroscope.

SPENDSpend Mercury Dollars to reduce your power bill or fly faster by converting them into Airpoints.

MERCURY DOLLARS1 Mercury Dollar is equivilent to 1 NZD, or 1 Airpoint.

YOUR DIGITAL LAKE LEVELStart spending, by dragging the scrubber down, & watch your lake level drop.

MY ACCOUNTWe worked closely with Mercury’s API team to improve & implement new 'My Account' functionality, allowing us to empower customers in different ways about their usage.

YOUR BILLView & pay your bill balance faster.

MULTIPLE ACCOUNTSEasily swap between your Mercury accounts.

USAGEMake smarter decisions about your power consumption by swipe & tapping the graphs to see your bill & daily usage breakdowns.

ONBOARDINGHelping customers explore & discover the app, so they can do the important things faster.

LOGIN SCREENSCustomer or not, get the bigger picture & see what Mercury Go has to offer, before needing to login.

DISCOVERY HELPERSTo help a customers discovery of Mercury Go & it's key functionality, "Discovery Helpers" are placed throughout.

We continue to plan & deliver a prioritised, outcome focused roadmap of continuous improvements, within 2 week sprint cycles.
